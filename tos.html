Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2023

1. Information We Collect

When you choose to log in with TikTok via FlowCast Studio, we receive limited information based on the permissions you grant:

  • Profile Info: Your TikTok display name and avatar URL (to verify your identity in our dashboard).
  • Content Data: Videos you explicitly upload to our dashboard for the purpose of publishing.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide the functionality of the service:

  • To display your logged-in status.
  • To upload and publish videos to your TikTok account upon your request via the TikTok API.

We do not sell your data or share it with third parties unrelated to the service functionality.

3. Data Retention

We only retain data necessary to provide the service. You may disconnect your TikTok account at any time via the TikTok app settings, which will revoke our access.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our support channels.
